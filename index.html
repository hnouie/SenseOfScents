Our Story

Founded on a love for scent

Sense of Scents Inc. was founded in June 2019 by Jeffrey P. Galang, inspired by the idea of celebrating the beauty and power of scents — the belief that fragrance can express emotions, boost confidence, and create a positive experience for everyone.

From a small scent maker, Sense of Scents has grown into a trusted, well-known brand now present in 19 SM malls across the Philippines, taking pride in its creativity, quality, and dedication to fragrances that suit every personality and lifestyle.

Our vision: sustainable craftsmanship and retail of distinct fragrances and scented products in the Philippines and abroad by 2030.
